## Expert Profiles

### Neil Patel
- **File:** [other/neil_patel.md](./other/neil_patel.md) · [youtube-transcripts/neil-patel/](./youtube-transcripts/neil-patel/)
- **Primary source:** [neilpatel.com/blog](https://neilpatel.com/blog) · NP Digital agency research · [YouTube — Neil Patel channel](https://www.youtube.com/@NeilPatel)
- **Key studies:**
  - NP Digital 5-month AI vs human content experiment (68 sites, 744 articles): AI averaged 52 visitors/month vs 283 for human-written; human outranked AI 94.12% of the time
  - 600-prompt hallucination study across 6 LLMs (565 marketers surveyed): 47.1% encounter AI inaccuracies multiple times/week; 36.5% have published hallucinated content
  - Content pruning study: removing low-quality posts produced 11–12% traffic lift
- **Videos Transcribed:**
  - [The Ranking System Behind ChatGPT & Google Gemini](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BOYPOMdZ0gk)
  - [The New Google Playbook: 8 Things You Must Fix Right Now](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Vk0pUUcVJI)
  - [How to Save Your Marketing Job (Stop Reporting Traffic)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rgyIkxhk1TY)
  - [You Don't Have an SEO Problem. You Have a "Brand Entity" Problem.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QNLdhW6Wxs4)
- **Annotation:** Co-founder of NP Digital (clients: Adobe, Lenovo, Samsung, Under Armour). Empirical, data-driven position on AI content workflows: AI handles research, outlines, drafts; human provides final editorial judgment. Covers GEO measurement skepticism (SparkToro study: <1/100 chance of same brand list in two AI responses), LLMs.txt implementation, and AI as workflow infrastructure ("directors of intelligence" vs. autonomous publisher model). Source types: blog research + video transcripts.

### Eric Enge
- **File:** [other/eric_enge.md](./other/eric_enge.md)
- **Primary source:** [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/author/eric-enge) · Perficient Digital / Pilot Holding
- **Key article:** [ChatGPT search vs. Google: A deep dive analysis of 62 queries](https://searchengineland.com/chatgpt-search-vs-google-analysis-449676) (Dec 23, 2024)
- **Annotation:** President of Pilot Holding, lead co-author of *The Art of SEO* (900+ pages, "the bible of SEO"), 2016 Search Marketer of the Year. Published rigorous 62-query comparison of ChatGPT search vs Google: Google wins overall (5.83 vs 5.19 informational; 6.44 vs 3.81 commercial; 6.25 vs 2.0 local); ChatGPT wins content gap analysis (3.25 vs 1.0). Core insight: Google's 20-year behavioral data advantage (Chrome 67.5% share, 118M Maps users) is structurally unreplicable. Expert review is non-negotiable for AI-generated content due to high error rates.

### Marie Haynes
- **File:** [other/marie_haynes.md](./other/marie_haynes.md)
- **Primary source:** [mariehaynes.com](https://mariehaynes.com) · [Search Bar Pro](https://mariehaynes.com/search-bar-pro/) newsletter
- **Annotation:** Former veterinarian turned SEO analyst, won Wix SEO competition 2019, author of *SEO in the Gemini Era*. Forensic reading of DOJ vs. Google trial testimony, Liz Reid's appeal declaration, and QRGs as primary sources. - **File:** [other/matt_cutts.md](./other/matt_cutts.md)
- **Primary source:** [mattcutts.com/blog](https://mattcutts.com/blog) · [Google Webmaster Central YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/user/GoogleWebmasterHelp)
- **Key posts:**

Guest blogging declaration (2014) illustrates the industrialization-to-spam cycle that AI content now accelerates.

### Rand Fishkin
- **File:** [other/rand_fishkin.md](./other/rand_fishkin.md)
- **Primary source:** [sparktoro.com](https://sparktoro.com) · [SparkToro blog](https://sparktoro.com/blog)
- **Key studies:**
  - SparkToro/Datos 2024 search data: Google grew 20%+ in 2024, ChatGPT has 373x fewer searches

Known for data-driven skepticism of vendor claims about AI search measurement.
